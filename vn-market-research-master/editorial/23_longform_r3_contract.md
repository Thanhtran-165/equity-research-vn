# Longform R3 editorial contract

## Product shape

- Master report: 15,000–18,000 canonical Vietnamese words.
- Five specialist reports: 4,000–6,000 canonical Vietnamese words each.
- Master stands alone; specialist reports deepen evidence rather than repeat the master.
- Every legacy Vercel report links to the master and to its corresponding master chapter.

## Reader contract

The primary reader is an investor without formal statistical training. Every empirical section follows this order:

1. The practical question in ordinary Vietnamese.
2. A concrete hypothetical example labelled as an illustration.
3. What the research observed.
4. What the observation means for an investor.
5. What must not be inferred.
6. Technical detail, only after the public explanation.

Technical terms may appear only after a plain-language definition. A number is never presented before the reader knows what question it answers. Statistical significance is not described as importance, and an in-sample relationship is not described as a forecast.

## Example taxonomy

- `MINH_HOA_GIA_DINH`: invented solely to explain an idea; never evidence.
- `VI_DU_TU_ARTIFACT`: derives from a governed result and carries a claim ID.
- `PHAN_VI_DU`: shows how the same visible state can lead to a different outcome.

Examples must not introduce new empirical frequencies, probabilities, causal mechanisms or historical episodes that are absent from governed artifacts.

## Visual contract

- Each visual answers one written question.
- Empirical visuals map to a governed claim and artifact.
- Conceptual diagrams say explicitly that they are explanatory, not measured frequencies.
- Captions state the permitted interpretation and the forbidden inference.
- No dual-axis causal implication, decorative test-count theatre, invented probabilities, buy/sell markers or hidden limitations.
- All labels and conclusions remain readable at 320px without hover.

## Cross-report consistency

The shared thesis is: data supports context, participation and disagreement checks; no independent, stable out-of-sample trading signal is confirmed. Each specialist report keeps its own scope and limitations. Results are compared narratively and never pooled numerically.

## Migration contract

Legacy deployments remain online during transition. Their canonical generators add a visible master-report banner. After the R3 specialist rewrite passes QA, each old project is redeployed in place. Projects are not deleted; old hashes and screenshots are preserved before replacement.
