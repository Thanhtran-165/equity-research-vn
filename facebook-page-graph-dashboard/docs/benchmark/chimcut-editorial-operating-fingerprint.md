## 5. Ideal Peer Candidate Profile

Based on the fingerprint, a strong Direct Core Peer should have:

1. **Topic overlap ≥ 3/5**: Stocks + macro + at least one of (gold/rates/RE/geopolitics)
2. **Video/reel heavy** (format similarity) OR strong text analysis with similar engagement patterns
3. **Independent creator or broker-KOL** — not pure corporate/institutional
4. **Scale 0.25×–10× of Chim Cút** (~11K–450K followers)
5. **Posting frequency ≥ 10/week** (comparable cadence)
6. **Public reactions + comments visible** (minimum for comparable engagement)
7. **Mix of analysis + commentary** — not pure news feed or pure entertainment

### Anti-pattern (what theanh98 was)

- Lifestyle/entrepreneurship creator with broad content
- Not stock-market-focused
- Different audience demographic
- Different operating model (personal brand, not market analysis)
